Purpose of this website

This is a BMI calculator project. I intend to create a simple webpage which will calculate BMI! The general chart to follow to check your BMI range are :

i) Below 18.5 = Underweight.

ii) Between 18.5-24.9 = Healthy!

iii) Between 25.0-29.9 = Overweight

iv) 30.0 and above = Obese!
